🚚 Delivery Route Optimizer PSO vs Genetic Algorithm

🚚 Delivery Route Optimization

Given a warehouse and multiple customer delivery locations, find the optimal route that minimizes total travel distance — reducing fuel cost and delivery time. This project uses two nature-inspired algorithms: Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) and Genetic Algorithm (GA) to solve this problem and compares their performance.

🌀 Particle Swarm Optimization

🐦

Inspired by: Flocking behavior of birds and schooling of fish

💡

Core Idea: A swarm of particles (solutions) fly through the search space. Each particle remembers its personal best position and is attracted toward the global best found by the swarm.

⚙️

Key Parameters: Number of particles, iterations, inertia weight, cognitive and social coefficients

✅

Strengths: Fast convergence, simple implementation, good for continuous problems

❌

Weaknesses: Can get stuck in local optima, less effective for discrete problems like routing

🧬 Genetic Algorithm

🦎

Inspired by: Darwinian evolution and natural selection

💡

Core Idea: A population of candidate solutions evolves over generations. Fitter solutions are selected for crossover and mutation, producing better offspring each generation.

⚙️

Key Parameters: Population size, generations, crossover rate, mutation rate

✅

Strengths: Good at escaping local optima, highly effective for discrete/combinatorial problems

❌

Weaknesses: Slower convergence, sensitive to parameter tuning
